About this tool

This visualization tool provides building-level energy performance data for the 2016, 2017, and 2018 calendar years for Philadelphia’s largest commercial and multifamily buildings, including around 2,700 buildings required to report annual energy and water usage as part of Philadelphia’s energy benchmarking law. You can learn more about energy benchmarking at www.phila.gov/benchmarking .
